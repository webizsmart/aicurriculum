## Phase 1: Product Discovery
1. Check for `DESIGN.md`. If it exists, ask to update, fresh start, or cancel.
2. Gather product context (purpose, users, category) from codebase and `/office-hours` outputs.
3. Determine if competitive research is required.

## Phase 2: Design Research & Synthesis
1. If research requested: identify competitors via WebSearch and perform visual audit via `browse` binary.
2. Perform Three-Layer Synthesis (Tried & True / New & Popular / First Principles).
3. Identify Eureka moments where conventional wisdom is wrong for this product.

## Phase 3: The Design System Proposal
1. Propose the complete coherent system: Aesthetic, Decoration, Layout, Color, Typography, Spacing, Motion.
2. Include the **SAFE/RISK Breakdown**: table stakes vs. deliberate departures to stand out.
3. Perform Coherence Validation on user adjustments.

## Phase 4: Visualization & Documentation
1. Generate the **Font & Color Preview Page** (self-contained HTML).
2. Iterate based on visual feedback until the system is approved.
3. Write `DESIGN.md` to the repository root as the source of truth.
4. Update `README.md` with instructions to follow `DESIGN.md`.
